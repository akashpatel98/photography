FAQ

Create | Shoot | Vision

Camera does not matter really. Your phone is the best camera. You carry around everywhere, easy to carry around. Currently all the phones camera picture quality is as good as a DSLR. When buying DSLR Canon's rebel series camera's are very good. They are not that expensive and comes with professional camera setting.

ISO is a camera setting which can brighten or darken a photo. As you increase your ISO, it will brighten the picture when clicked. It can help where there in darker environments. However, raising ISO too much will show lots of grain also known as noise.

ND filter blocks the light in a neutral way without changing the color of the light. It is very useful when shooting Landscape photography. As it provides more creative control over shutter speed. Normally ISO is set to the lowest setting and aperture between f/11 or f/16.

There is few things one can do in low light. Increase ISO Setting, Use slower shutter speed and adjust the aperture. Additionally try to reduce a camera shake. Use Off camera flashes (OCD) which provide good lighting for the picture.

Shooting in auto mode basically means camera controls all your setting while you just click a picture. Shooting in manual is very different as you have more control over all settings. You get more creative with camera. It helps you understand your camera better.
