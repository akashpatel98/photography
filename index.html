About me

Hello, my name is Akash Patel. I started taking pictures 5 years ago. I always had passion for it. Started with iphone camera to now using DSLR. It has been an amazing journey of learning and putting skills to work. It challenges you everyday to be more creative. At first i used to watch youtube to learn basics as i did not know anyother medium to learn from. I struggled in the beginning but i learned a lot in the last 5 years. This website is for everyone who wants to learn photography. I have put in what i learned through what i learned so far and additional information to get started.
